About Monarch Shopping

Your one-stop shop for fashion, accessories, and more.

Who We Are

Monarch Shopping is an online retail platform dedicated to bringing you quality fashion, accessories, and lifestyle items at affordable prices. Established in 2025, we are a student-driven project committed to merging style and convenience in one seamless shopping experience.

Our Mission

To provide customers nationwide with trendy, high-quality, and budget-friendly products, while ensuring a smooth and reliable shopping experience from browsing to checkout.

Our Team

This project was proudly built by BSIS 1C 2025 students: CAYANES, GUARTE, and BULAONG. We aim to combine our technical skills with creative vision to build impactful digital experiences.
